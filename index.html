My Story

Three years have passed since my first job. I pursued a degree in Industrial Engineering and tried to continue in the field, but I began to feel the repetition of routine — as if my knowledge had stopped growing.

I followed my heart and my true passion: programming. Resigning wasn't easy, especially with a child and monthly expenses to pay.

I joined a bootcamp to deepen my knowledge. The feeling of doing what I truly love is indescribable — I'm still eager to learn and improve every single day.
